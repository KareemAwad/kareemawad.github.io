Visualization and Analysis of Unstructured Data

CANDEV Data Challenge - Satistics Canada

Using Python, Pandas and Tableau, our team cleaned and analysed over 40,000 data points for the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat to create a visualization that would help explore and understand how programs related to business innovation and growth support would change over time.

Waterfront Robotics

Computer Aided Design Project - University of Ottawa

Designed a 6-axis articulated robot as part of a waterfront waste removal initiative, to be able to traverse various terrain autonomously in order to locate and remove litter and store for disposal. The robot was designed and modelled using SolidWorks and MATLAB.
